About Us


TeaAmor Café was launched in 2018

Since its launch, it has received a lot of attention from the entire community. This cafe is in Shah Alam, Selangor. This area is a hotspot area close to employment buildings and a residential area that is the focus of families. It was originally a simple cafe. In the beginning, our intention was to establish this cafe to introduce to the people the privilege of tea in our cafe. For most people, they consider tea to be a common drink compared to coffee. we want to change this perception. TeaAmor Café is a cup of tea that can be enjoyed at any time. This can also be a fan of non-coffee lovers. TeaAmor Café provides a variety of teabased drinks that have their own uniqueness. It can be enjoyed either cold or hot. With high quality ingredients, as well as sophisticated teamakers, we want to provide quality drinks at reasonable prices for everyone. With sales of 2 million cups sold since the opening of this cafe, we thank our Amore’ for this achievement. In this cafe we useherbal ingredients.

This company is based on trust in ensuring that all products run smoothly and are of good quality and provide solid service to customers. In addition, prioritizing the welfare of employees and the team to achieve the company's vision and mission with excellence. The quote of our company is by "Indulge in the art of relaxation, one sip at a time". We aim to provide uniqueness and experience with every sip of our drinks and the taste of the snacks. Being surrounded by friendly and kind staff, as well as a cozy and comfortable atmosphere, as if our customers are at home and calm by just enjoying our drinks. TeaAmor Café focuses on tea first and foremost. We offer over 30 different varieties of tea by experts and are made from the best tea leaves, combined to suit the customer. The high and excellent standards of our foods will always guarantee satisfaction through fresh and quality ingredients.
