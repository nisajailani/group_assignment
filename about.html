About Us


TeaAmor Café was launched in 2015

The entire community has given it a lot of attention since it was introduced. This cafe is in Shah Alam, Selangor. Near employment buildings and a residential area that attracts families, this region is a hotspot. At first, it was just a basic cafe. Our original goal in starting this café was to acquaint the public to the luxury of tea at our establishment. In contrast to coffee, most people view tea as a common beverage. Our goal is to alter this impression. You can have a cup of tea at any moment with TeaAmor Café. This can also be a fan of people who don't drink coffee. A range of distinctive tea-based beverages are offered by TeaAmor Café. Both hot and cold versions are tasty. With premium ingredients and advanced teamakers, we want to offer everyone high-quality drinks at affordable costs. We are grateful to our Amore' for this accomplishment, since we have sold two million cups since the cafe's opening. We now have over 5 stores across Malaysia which is located at Jitra, Johor Bharu and Kuala Terengganu and are the leading tea brand in Southeast Asia thanks to strong sales achievements. In this cafe we use herbal ingredients.

This business is founded on the belief that all products should function properly, be of high quality, and offer clients reliable service. Additionally, putting the team's and employees' well-being first to successfully accomplish the company's vision and objective. "Indulge in the art of relaxation, one sip at a time" is the quote that our company uses. With each sip of our beverages and snack taste, we want to offer something special and unforgettable. being surrounded by welcoming and courteous staff and a warm, inviting ambiance that makes our patrons feel at home and at ease while simply sipping our drinks. Tea is the primary emphasis of TeaAmor Café. Expertly crafted from the finest tea leaves, we provide more than 30 distinct tea kinds to meet the tastes of our customers. Using only the best and freshest ingredients, our dishes' high standards will always ensure your delight.
